HOW TO PLAY

🎮 Controls

• WASD / Arrow Keys — Move

• Mouse — Aim (auto-fires)

• Left Click — Manual shoot

• SPACE — Dash toward mouse

• R — Special ability

• 1-9 — Admin abilities (Admin only!)

• F11 / ⛶ Button — Fullscreen

⭐ Objective

• Survive waves of enemies

• Kill enemies to gain EXP

• Level up and pick upgrades

• Grab green/purple pickups

💰 Ways to Earn Money

• $1 per round survived (global currency)

• $5 per boss killed (global currency)

• $2 per 10 kills streak bonus

• $10 per level up / $50 at level 10

• 💰 1 Gold per kill (run currency for wave shop!)

• 🪙 Gold Hoarder character earns 5x ALL money (terrible fighter!)
